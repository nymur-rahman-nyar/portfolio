Antojal Website Project

Created: Nov 2020

Overview:

  • Project Objective: Developed a website called Antojal using WordPress during the pandemic to connect teachers and students, aiming to create a platform for educational collaboration.
  • Platform: WordPress with a custom template
  • Design Work: Personally designed all icons and backgrounds, adding a unique visual identity to the site.
  • User Base: Initially attracted 30 users, including freelancers and teachers, providing a space for educational engagement.
  • Marketing and Promotion: Promoted the website on Facebook and YouTube using targeted ads on Google Ads and Facebook Ads platforms. Created a tutorial video using specialized software to guide users through the site.

Challenges and Resolution:

  • Ransomware Attack: Faced a ransomware attack but successfully restored the website from a backup, ensuring data integrity and minimal downtime.
  • Outcome: Despite the initial traction, the site was eventually closed due to challenges in scaling the user base and customer acquisition.
